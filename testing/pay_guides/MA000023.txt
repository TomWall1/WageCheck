When do the rates in the pay guide apply from? The first full pay period starting on or after 07 October 2025. What is in this pay guide? It's a summary of the award rates. Go to the award for information about coverage, classifications, conditions and the operation of minimum rates, allowances, penalty rates and overtime. Where can I go for more information? Our Pay and Conditions Tool can help you work out pay rates, leave, notice and redundancy minimum entitlements based on the information you provide.
